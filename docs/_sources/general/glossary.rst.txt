Glossary
===============

.. glossary::
  Tool Material
    gold, iron, etc.

  Datapack
    Vanilla mod for Minecraft

  Workstation
    Where tool is put and where you stand

  Activation
    Triggering a grind

  Region
    A biome or mine

  Biome
    Minecraft term for biome

  Mine
    Datapack term for activaing below level 55 (at the time of writing)
